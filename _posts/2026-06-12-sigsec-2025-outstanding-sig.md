title: SIG SEC Named Outstanding SIG for 2025
---

The Association for Information Systems has recognized SIG Information Security and Privacy (SIG SEC) as an AIS Outstanding SIG for 2025. The designation is awarded annually to SIGs, chapters, and colleges that have made exceptional contributions to the IS community.

This recognition belongs to our members. The work you do — submitting research, participating in workshops, reviewing papers, and showing up year after year — is what makes this SIG worth recognizing.

We'll be displaying the AIS Outstanding Community badge on our website and communications going forward. AIS will also announce the full list of honored communities in an upcoming issue of the AIS InSider.

Thank you for being part of SIG SEC. Here's to another strong year.
